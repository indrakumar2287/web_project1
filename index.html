SM School is a well-known educational institution that is located in the heart of the city. Established in the year 1985, the school has a rich history of providing quality education to students from different parts of the country. The school has a sprawling campus that is equipped with all the modern facilities required for the holistic development of students. The classrooms are spacious and well-ventilated, providing a comfortable learning environment for students. The school also has well-equipped laboratories, a library, and a playground where students can engage in various sports and extracurricular activities. SM School has a team of highly qualified and experienced teachers who are dedicated to providing the best possible education to students. The teachers use innovative teaching methods to make learning more engaging and interactive for students. The school offers education from Nursery to Class XII and follows the CBSE curriculum. SM School has a strong alumni network, with many of its former students holding positions of importance in various fields. The school also has tie-ups with reputed colleges and universities, which ensures that its students are well-prepared for higher education. Overall, SM School is a great choice for parents who are looking for a school that provides quality education, a nurturing environment, and opportunities for holistic development.
